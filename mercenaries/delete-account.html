Delete Your Account

Last updated: July 22, 2026

You can ask us to delete your Mercenaries account and the data associated with it at any time. This page explains what gets deleted and how to request it.

How to request deletion

Email us from the address you use to sign in to Mercenaries, so we can verify the request:

Email: beardedbeardeveloper@gmail.com

Subject: “Mercenaries account deletion request”

Email a deletion request

We’ll confirm by reply and complete the deletion within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes, from our servers:

  • Your account identifier and sign-in details (including your email address and any linked Google/Apple identity).
  • Your username (display name).
  • Your cloud-saved game — decks, campaign progress, and statistics.

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

What stays on your device

Mercenaries keeps a local copy of your progress on your device so you can play offline. Deleting your account does not erase that local copy. To remove it, clear the app’s data or uninstall the game on your device.

Playing without an account

If you never signed in, we hold no account or data for you to delete — your progress exists only on your device. Clearing the app’s data or uninstalling removes it.
