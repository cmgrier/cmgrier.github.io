Mercenaries

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Mercenaries (“the game”, “we”, “us”) is a mobile card-battler published by Bearded Bear Studios. This policy explains what data the game handles, why, and the choices you have. It covers the Mercenaries app (com.beardedbearstudios.mercenaries) and its online services.

The short version. You can play the entire game without an account, and in that case nothing about you leaves your device. If you choose to create an account, we store the minimum needed to back up your progress and let you play online: an account ID, your sign-in email, a display name, and your saved game. We don’t sell your data, show ads, or use third-party advertising or analytics trackers.

1. When we collect data

The game is fully playable offline and signed-out. In that mode your decks, campaign progress, and settings live only on your device and are never sent to us. We begin storing data off your device only when you create or sign in to an account (Email, Google, or Apple) to back up and sync your progress.

3. Online multiplayer

Online matches connect the two players directly (peer-to-peer). To set up that connection, a signaling server briefly relays connection details between the two devices; it never sees your deck, your moves, or the match result. Your device’s IP address is used to negotiate the connection and, where a direct link isn’t possible, match traffic is routed through a relay server so the two players’ IP addresses stay hidden from each other. Match traffic is encrypted.

4. Who processes your data

We keep the number of third parties to a minimum, and only ones that act as service providers on our behalf:

  • Supabase — hosts our authentication and database, i.e. your account and your cloud save.
  • Connection servers (STUN/relay) — used transiently to connect online matches, as described above.

We do not sell your data or share it for advertising.

5. Security

Data sent between the game and our services is encrypted in transit (HTTPS, and the multiplayer connection is encrypted end-to-end). Account and save data stored with Supabase is encrypted at rest, and access is restricted so that a given account’s save can only be read or changed by that account.

6. Data retention

We keep your account and cloud save for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account (see below), the associated data is removed.

7. Your choices and rights

  • Play without an account — nothing is stored off your device.
  • Delete your account and data — see our Account Deletion page.
  • Access or export your data — email us and we’ll provide a copy of the data associated with your account.

8. Children

Mercenaries is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the game evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
