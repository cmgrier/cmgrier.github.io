Mercenaries

A dark-fantasy card-battler. Recruit a warband, master your deck, and fight your way through the campaign — or duel other players online.

Coming soon to Google Play

Mercenaries is a mobile tactical card game. Assemble a company of hired blades, build a deck around their abilities, and outfight your opponents one turn at a time. Play solo against the campaign or take your warband online.

What’s inside

  • Build your warbandCollect mercenaries and champions, then craft decks around how their abilities combo.
  • Campaign & roguelike runsFight through a solo campaign with progression, unlocks, and run-based challenges.
  • Online duelsMatch against other players in direct, encrypted peer-to-peer battles.
  • Play your wayFully playable offline — sign in only when you want to back up and sync your progress across devices.
